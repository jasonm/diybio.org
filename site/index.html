An institution for the amateur.

DIYbio is an organization for the ever expanding community fo citizen scientists and DIY biological engineers that value openness & responsibility.

We aim to provide the same resources afforded by more traditional instituions, such as access to a community of experts, to technical literature and other resources, and to responsible oversight for health and safety.

Get Informed

Learn about our mission, our committment to safety and ethics, and watch an interview with community members about their thoughts.

Get Excited

Watch videos about our featured projects, including our plans for bootstrapped public wetlabs, global FlashLab experiments, and our innovation of next-gen lab equipment.

Participate

New to biology, or a seasoned pro? Software hacker or PCR extraordinaire? Get involved by diving into a project, starting something new, or finding a meetup near you.

Donate

Help bring the technical, safety, and community resources that are available to world-class institutions to innovators and enthusiasts worldwide.
