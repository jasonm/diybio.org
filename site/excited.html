FlashLab1: BioWeatherMaps

Flash mobs meet consumer-generated science in the new DIYbio initiative FlashLabs, where we'll be pulling-off a new large-scale collaborative science project annually for amateurs and enthusiasts worldwide. First up: the BioWeatherMap. Ever wonder how the microbial communities living on cross-walk buttons in Boston compare to those in San Francisco, or Manhattan, or the cross-walk nearest your home? We're going to find out and you can get involved. Self-assembly required!

SmartLab

Hardware hacking lab instruments, large-format multitouch displays, and a physical computing interface with real-time sample tracking and data logging meets a beautiful and natural way for scientists to learn, research, visualize, and share their work. Intelligent and interactive protocol guides, intuitive data manipulation and transformation, and livestreaming your science to an electronic lab notebook are a few components of this cross-disciplinary project. And go open source or go home!
