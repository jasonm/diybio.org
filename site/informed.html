What does DIYbio mean to me?

Are you a lab tech who loves to teach? A passionate autodidact, a hands-on tinkerer? An educator with an eye toward low-cost biotech equipment and hands-on lessons? An innovator with the next revolution in bioremediation or biologically-sourced alternative energy? Or perhaps a community member, author, or report who is interested in how amateur science affects you?

Find out how we can help you, and those around you, explore biology in a safe, responsible, and ethical manner by providing open access to technical literature, safety and legal resources, and the oversign of an expert safety advisory board.
